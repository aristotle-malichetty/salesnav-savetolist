# Privacy Policy for SalesNav Lead Saver

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2025

## Overview

SalesNav Lead Saver is a Chrome extension that helps users save leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator to custom lists. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

This extension collects and stores **minimal data**, all of which remains on your local device:

| Data | Purpose | Storage Location |
|------|---------|------------------|
| Last used list name | Auto-fill convenience | Local browser storage |
| Automation state | Resume after page navigation | Local browser storage |

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- LinkedIn credentials or login data
- Lead data or contact information
- Browsing history
- Analytics or usage data
- Any data sent to external servers

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. **No data is transmitted to any external servers.**

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. The extension operates entirely on your local machine.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `activeTab` | To interact with the current LinkedIn Sales Navigator tab |
| `storage` | To save your last used list name and automation state locally |
| `linkedin.com` | To run the extension only on LinkedIn Sales Navigator pages |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

## Data Retention

Data is stored locally until:
- You clear your browser data
- You uninstall the extension
- You manually clear the extension's storage

## Your Rights

You can:
- **View stored data**: Go to `chrome://extensions`, click "Details" on the extension, then "Extension storage"
- **Delete all data**: Uninstall and reinstall the extension, or clear browser data
- **Stop data collection**: Simply don't use the extension

## Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for professional use and is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
